Cookies

Description

Cookies are a classic homemade treat that are easy to make and perfect for any occasion. They're crispy on the outside, soft on the inside, and full of comforting flavors. This recipe is great for beginners and can be customized with different add-ins like chocolate chips or nuts. Freshly baked cookies are best enjoyed warm with a glass of milk.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Unsalted butter
  • Granulated sugar
  • Brown sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Mix the dry ingredients together in a bowl.
  3. In a separate bowl, cream the butter and sugars until smooth.
  4. Add the eggs and vanilla extract, mixing well.
  5. Slowly combine the wet and dry ingredients.
  6. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  7. Scoop dough onto the baking sheet, spacing them apart.
  8. Bake until the edges are lightly golden.
  9. Remove from the oven and let the cookies cool.
  10. Serve and enjoy.
