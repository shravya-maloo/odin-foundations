Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian comfort dish made with layers of pasta, rich sauce, creamy cheese, and flavorful fillings. It's hearty, filling, and perfect for family dinners or special occasions. This recipe is simple enough for beginners but still delivers a warm, homemade taste. The layers come together to create a dish that's both satisfying and full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Olive oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ground meat or vegetables (your choice)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven and prepare a baking dish.
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan and cook the onion and garlic until soft.
  4. Add the ground meat or vegetables and cook until fully done.
  5. Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, and seasonings, then let it simmer.
  6. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheeses in the baking dish.
  7. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used.
  8. Finish with a layer of cheese on top.
  9. Bake until the lasagna is hot and bubbly.
  10. Let it cool slightly before serving.
